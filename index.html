For me, 3D animation isn't just a career; it's a journey of discovery that kicked off over a decade ago right here in Colombia. I started out as a 3D Generalist, juggling all sorts of fast-paced challenges and learning on the fly. This broad foundation has been my superpower, making me incredibly adaptable and ready for any challenge a team throws my way, whether it's figuring out a tricky technical hurdle or just needing someone who can wear many hats.

Then spent few years diving into the magic of 3D Feature Animation, learning the ropes of bringing grand stories to life where i got the change to collaborate in my very first movie!.

My journey have taken me through some cool projects: a shipped game title, a feature film, various TV shows, and a bunch of commercials. I've had the chance to grow from an Animator to senior and even an Lead Animator, shaping entire creative visions. Now, I'm deep into exploring Unreal Engine, always pushing to blend technical excellence with the kind of exaggeration that makes animation truly pop.

When I'm not making characters jump, slash, or emote, you’ll probably find me out cycling to clear my head. And if movies come up, fair warning: I might geek out about Interstellar. I'm always up for a creative challenge or just swapping ideas with other passionate folks.
